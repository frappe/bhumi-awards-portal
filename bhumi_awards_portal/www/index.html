Responsible Campus Awards

India's first-ever awards to recognize socially responsible colleges. One-of-its-kind attempt to learn from and strengthen Indian college's focus on building the future change-makers of this country.

Vote Now

Why are we hosting these awards?

Promoting‌

Promoting the spirit of doing good and volunteerism among Tamil Nadu colleges.

Learning

Learning from the most socially conscious colleges and amplifying practices in other colleges.

Empowering

Accelerate the effort to empower youth in India to play a stronger role in building our nation.

Process

How do we do this?

1. Nominations

The awards team invites applications. Colleges can self-nominate themselves. The public or students can also nominate/vote for specific colleges.

2. Applications

Nominated colleges are reached out to and asked to submit details in a detailed form about their effort and initiatives to be socially conscious.

3. Selection

An expert crew of jury would be evaluating the nominations based on fixed criteria and details submitted by the college. Final awardees are selected based on the college’s score in specific criteria and the number of public votes.

4. Award Ceremony

All the nominated colleges and the awardees are invited for the final award ceremony where awards are given and guest speeches are delivered on making Indian colleges more socially responsible.

Criteria

The following are the criteria with which the jury members will be evaluating the nominations from colleges. Specifics about each criteria bucket and details would be decided by the expert panel consisting of subject matter experts & NGOs.
